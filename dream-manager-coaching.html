What Dream Manager Coaching Is

A Structured Methodology, Not Life Coaching

One-on-one work with a Certified Dream Manager to identify, plan, and pursue the dreams that matter most to you — across twelve domains of life. Career transitions. Financial goals. Creative projects. Adventure. Legacy. Health. Relationships. The stuff that doesn't fit on a performance review.

This is a structured methodology, not life coaching. It's based on the work of Matthew Kelly and Floyd Consulting, and it's been used inside hundreds of companies to help employees design lives they actually want. Now it's available without an employer in the picture.

How It Works

From First Conversation to Living Your Dreams

1

Discovery Call

30 minutes. We talk about where you are, where you want to be, and whether this is the right fit.

2

Dream Identification

A structured process across 12 life domains uncovers the dreams you've been carrying — and some you didn't know you had until someone asked.

3

Action Planning

For each dream that matters, we build a specific plan with milestones and a timeline.

4

Regular Sessions

Bi-weekly or monthly virtual sessions to work the plan, navigate obstacles, and keep the momentum. You get access to DreamCompass — the platform I built for tracking dreams and milestones — throughout the program.
